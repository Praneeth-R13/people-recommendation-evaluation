# Summary
Metro Water Recovery (Metro) is seeking a utility management consultant to provide strategic energy planning and utility management advisory services for its Thermal Energy Program Support Project. The primary objective is to advance organizational goals of reducing effluent temperatures, recovering thermal energy from wastewater, and exploring opportunities to monetize this resource. The selected firm will assist in developing financial and business strategies, identifying strategic partnerships, and implementing infrastructure projects to achieve these objectives.

# Scope
The selected Owner's Advisor (OA) will provide multi-year advisory services to Metro, translating its vision into actionable projects. This includes management guidance, technical expertise, and strategic support for effluent temperature reduction, wastewater thermal energy recovery, and thermal energy monetization. The OA will identify opportunities, ensure program alignment, and integrate technical, financial, and regulatory considerations across all thermal initiatives. Key components include co-developing a multi-year strategic roadmap, preparing a formal program framework, and guiding Metro in developing its technical strategy, financial strategy, utility ownership models, policy support, and stakeholder engagement. The OA will also manage subconsultants and assist in procurement processes as needed.
